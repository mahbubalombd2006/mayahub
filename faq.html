Frequently Asked Questions

Maya Hub is a freelancer marketplace for connecting clients and professionals.
Is it free?
Yes, Maya Hub is free for both freelancers and clients.

You can join by creating a profile and listing your services.
Can I hire freelancers?
Yes, clients can directly contact freelancers.
Is payment handled?
No, payment is handled directly between client and freelancer.
Can I update profile?
Yes, you can update your profile anytime.
Is my data safe?
Yes, we follow standard privacy protection methods.
How to contact support?
Use the contact page to reach support team.
Can I delete account?
Yes, account deletion is possible anytime.
Do you verify freelancers?
We recommend checking profiles before hiring.
Is there mobile support?
Yes, Maya Hub is fully mobile responsive.
Can I list services?
Yes, freelancers can list all services.
Is there commission?
Currently no commission is charged.
Can I contact multiple freelancers?
Yes, you can contact anyone.
Is login required?
No login is required for browsing.
Can I report user?
Yes, report option will be added soon.
Do you support global users?
Yes, it is a global platform.
Can I advertise service?
Yes, freelancers can promote services.
Is support 24/7?
Support availability depends on system updates.
Will Maya Hub grow?
Yes, we are continuously improving the platform.
